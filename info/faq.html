What does the price include?

All facilities listed in the room descriptions are included in the room price. To see the facilities, just click on the hotel name. You can see if breakfast or anything else, are included. For all our products listed, taxes are already included in price.

Can I make a reservation without a credit card?

No. A valid credit card is required at the time of booking, which is charged the full amount. For all services, our partners require this. In addition, the credit card used must be the same as the individual making the reservation.

How do I know if my reservation is confirmed?

As soon as you've completed the booking process, a confirmation page will appear. This page shows all of your reservation details, including the booking number and your generated log in information, so you can access your confirmation online. We'll also send you a confirmation email with all of your booking information. "On Request" bookings are not confirmed immediately and you will be contacted directly for these reservations.

How We Protect and Secure Your Credit Card Information

Payment in full is required at the time of booking. Payment should be made through Executive Travel Link's website or by phone with a credit or debit card.

How can I see how much a hotel room costs?

Once you have entered the dates of your stay, the rates will be clearly displayed along with the available room types. Rates displayed are including all taxes. If booking more than 1 room in the same search, the average rate displayed is the cost of all rooms combined.

I am entering my credit card details. When will I be charged?

For all reesrvations made with Executive Travel Link, payment is required immediately processing the reservation. Our partners require this which is how we are able to provide deep discounts in many cases. Incomplete or "on request" reservations are not charged until a voucher is issued.

We have children; can we get extra beds/cribs in the room?

Information regarding children and extra beds/cribs can be found under "Hotel Policies." Please take note of the following:

Any additional fees for children are included in the reservation price. Special requests for cribs or rollaway beds should be emailed to us at 'reservations@executivetravellink.com'. We recommend calling the hotel prior to your arrival to guarantee your request. You can find the hotel’s contact information in your confirmation email. You can always contact us to confirm availability before you book.

Can I make a booking for tonight on your website?

Yes, you can! Just be sure to take extra care when entering all of your information (especially your credit card number) as we may not have enough time to contact you to fix any errors, which could jeopardize your reservation. Also, be careful that the hotel you select is "Available" and not "On Request"; same day bookings on request are more difficult to confirm and get ahold of you before arrival.

Can I search for hotels by price?

Yes. By default the hotels are sorted by cheapest price, with the some of the best deals on top. You can also sort by Name, Availability, Recommended.

Can I limit the search to show hotels in a specific district or near a landmark?

After the initial search, you can 'Modify Results' on the left to narrow down area and district such as Downtown or Disney Area, etc.

How can I search for hotels that have rooms large enough for families?

When searching for hotels you will need to enter the occupying adults and children, from here the only hotels that will display to you will be those that have available configuration for your family.

We have children; can we get extra beds/cribs in the room?

Information regarding children and extra beds/cribs can be found under "Hotel Details." Please take note of the following:

During the online booking process, please make your request for any extra bed/crib by emailing us at reservations@executivetravellink.com.

We recommend calling the hotel prior to your arrival to guarantee your request. You can find the hotel’s contact information in your confirmation email.

You can always contact us to confirm availability before you book.

What is the difference between a Double Room and a Twin Room?

A Double Room has one double/full bed and a Twin Room has 2 twin beds. If a room is called Double/Twin, it can be set up for either type. You can specify your bed-type preference by selecteding the "Twin" checkbox in the search box.

Are the prices shown per person or per room?

The price we show is for the room for the entire length of the stay, unless otherwise stated in the room type and description.

Are taxes included in the price?

Yes. The prices shown are always including taxes and no additional charges will happen during checkout. However, some hotels may have a resort fee which is payable directly to them.

Is breakfast included in the price?

This depends on the hotel and the room type. You can see if it's included by checking the text just below the room name.

Do I pay a reservation fee to Executive Travel Link?

No, we don't charge any fees at all.

Can I use discount coupons (e.g. issued by magazines, stores, etc.)?

No, you cannot use discount coupons when booking on our website. In such cases you will need to follow the instructions given by the organization issuing the coupon.

Can I pay for my stay at the hotel with a different credit card than the one used to make the reservation?

No. The contracts we have with our partners require the reservation to be fully prepaid to Executive Travel Link at time of the booking; so it is not possible to pay directly at the hotel.

How can I make a special request?

For example: a sea view, twin beds, room on a certain floor or dietary needs?

Once you have received your booking confirmation, you can follow up with the hotel directly with us to confirm any special requests. In many cases, room type such as view or bedding will be defined.

Can I book a hotel for day use?

No, bookings are for a one night minimum stay only. If you are looking for meeting space or facility rooms please contact us directly at reservations@executivetravellink.com.

How do I find out how many bedrooms are in an apartment/suite?

The number of bedrooms should be clearly stated in the room description. If the information is not there, or is unclear, you can always contact us. If you have already made your reservation, you can contact the hotel directly with the contact details provided in your confirmation email.

How can I make a group reservation?

Many hotels on our website can accommodate groups. Please note that the number of rooms assigned to a group varies for each hotel and that special conditions may apply for group bookings. Contact us directly for any group inquiries.

I have booked a hotel but haven't received a confirmation by email. What should I do?

Try looking in the spam/junk folders as well as the inbox of the email address attached to your booking. You can also use the booking number and log in information from the confirmation page to log on and access a copy of the confirmation email. As always, feel free to contact us if you need any further assistance.

Are the facilities in the room that I have booked guaranteed?

Yes, the room facilities listed under each room type are guaranteed by the hotel.

If I need to cancel my booking will I pay a cancellation fee?

Cancellation policies vary from room to room. You can usually cancel or change your booking up to 24 hours before your planned arrival time. You can check to see what a hotel’s policy is, and whether they will charge a cancellation fee, under "Hotel Policies." This information is also included in your confirmation email. Please note that if your room is non-refundable or part of a special deal, it may have a different policy. Executive Travel Link will never charge a fee for cancel, but if you are inside of cancellation period there is typically a one-night room and tax charge.

How do I know if my booking has been canceled?

Once you have canceled your reservation you should receive an email confirming the cancellation. Please check your inbox and spam/junk mail folders. If you don’t receive an email please contact us.
