Salt Lake City Features

The capital of Utah, Salt Lake City is a densely populated area with a population that at last count in 2010 totaled upwards of 186,440 people. The Avenues is one of the local neighborhoods in Salt Lake City considered to be off the city’s grid map system. The Avenues is classified as a historical district and is classified as an entirely populated residential neighborhood. There are many Victorian era homes in this area, located just northeast of downtown Salt Lake City. Enjoy some of the city’s culture and nightlife. If you enjoy skiing and snowboarding you’re in the right place. Hit the slopes at one of several local ski resorts. Take in some local history and visit the Marmalade district.

Getting Around in Salt Lake City

Salt Lake City has a variety of transportation modes, but the most popular way of getting around the city of course is by car. There are several airport shuttle services that local hotels offer to their guests as a complimentary service. The Utah transit authority is the city’s source for mass transit and is in charge of the busses and the TRAX light-rail system found throughout the Salt Lake area.

Hotel Deals in Salt Lake City

Discount Hotels

Located in downtown Salt Lake City, the Chase Suite Hotel offers 24 hour front desk services and around the clock room service. The hotel offers one bedroom suites that include a living room with sofa sleeper, and rooms with are fireplaces available upon request.
The Comfort Inn Downtown is right in the heart of Salt Lake City. With excellent recommendations from satisfied guests, the Comfort Inn combines great value with excellent amenities and an excellent house staff. The rooms offered by the Comfort Inn Downtown include LCD TVs and also in room microwave ovens.

Mid-Range Hotels

For a different lodging experience there is the Anton Boxrud Bed & Breakfast Inn. This Victorian style bed and breakfast has 7 different rooms to choose from. Some rooms offer California King Beds and unique vaulted ceilings, and whirlpool bath tubs. The inn is just 15 minutes away from the downtown area. The Hampton Inn and Suites Ogden is centrally located in Ogden, and is just a few minutes away from the George S. Eccles Dinosaur Park and the Ogden Utah temple. The hotel has a full fitness center, and has a business center where small business meetings can be scheduled. The rooms in the Hampton offer coffee and tea makers in each room, televisions with premium cable channels and Wi-Fi internet access and queen and king sized beds.

Luxury Hotels

One of the city’s most celebrated hotels, the Grand America Hotel, is a stately building with its 24 stories rising above the salt lake landscape. Some of the amenities that you will find here are executive king suites that feature king sized beds, balconies that you can step out onto, and 24 hour in room dining services.
The St. Regis Deer Valley is situated on the slopes of the Wasatch Mountains, and is a short distance from the home of the Sundance film festival, historic park city. The guestrooms offer beautiful décor and each room depending on the type that you select offers slopeside views, fireplaces, a gourmet kitchen and spectacular views of the Wasatch Mountains.
