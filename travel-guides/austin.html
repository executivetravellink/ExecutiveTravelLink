February - March; September - November

Reasonably priced hotels for unrehearsed journeys to Austin are next to impossible to get in February (Austin Marathon), March (South by Southwest), in the work of UT footy season, and in the work of the Austin City Limits Music Festival (October) unless you plan far in advance (at least six months).

June - August; December - January

Austin weather gets hot here in the summertime, which makes for a miserable time of walking around & exploring the sights unless you sit in Barton Springs all day. For that reason, June through August is not the best time to visit Austin. In case you must visit in the work of the summer, plan your trip in June, when it's not (usually) as hot. December & January run in to the holidays, so you can usually find some lovely hotel deals & packages.

April - May

Early spring (well after South by Southwest in March) is of the best times to visit Austin. Hotel prices & travel deals are more reasonable, all the SXSW out-of-towners have mostly left, & the bars & restaurants aren't as crowded. & it's not blazing hot yet which is always a benefit.
