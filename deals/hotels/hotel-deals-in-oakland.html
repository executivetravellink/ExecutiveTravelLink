Oakland Features

One of the greatest cities to visit is Oakland. There are many attractions, entertainment venues and dining options to visit while in this fabulous city. Such activities include the Oakland Museum of California, Fairyland, USS Potomac, the Paramount Theatre & the Oakland Zoo. The Oakland Museum of California is well known for its re-created environments rather than their exhibits. They offer magnificent arts & nature from all over California. Fairyland is a great place for children to use their imaginations. Not only that but they have small rides, animals and play sets. The USS Potomac is a great museum to check out because it was originally the "Floating White House", according to Franklin D. Roosevelt. A beautiful theatre to visit while in Oakland is the Paramount Theatre; it is a phenomenal, restored art deco. Lastly, Oakland offers a great experience at the Oakland Zoo.

Getting Around in Oakland

Since Oakland is a big city, it will be pretty easy to get around using public transportation. By taking the BART (Bay Area Rapid Transit) you will be able to reach most parts of the city, even nearby cities such as San Francisco.

Hotel Deals in Oakland

Discount Hotels

Quality Inn and the La Quinta Inn are two great hotel deals in Oakland. The Quality Inn is well known for their mouthwatering breakfast & clean rooms. This hotel is pet-friendly and also nonsmoking. The La Quinta is another great selection because of its location. It is located right next to the Oakland Coliseum and 2 miles from the Oakland International Airport. Downtown Oakland is also 2 miles away. Another great service they offer is their free 24 hour service shuttle. It runs to and from the airport and anywhere within 2 miles of the hotel.

Mid-Range Hotels

Two other great hotels to stay at while in Oakland are the Executive Inn & Suites and the Clarion Hotel. Located 5 minutes away from Downtown Oakland, 15 minutes away from the airport and 20 minutes away from San Francisco is the Executive Inn & Suites. They provide a shuttle service that will take you to the airport, Downtown and the Jack London Square. The Clarion Hotel is located near all the attractions that the Bay Area has to offer, including Napa and Sonoma Wine Country, the East Bay, Berkeley, and San Francisco is just 12 minutes away.

Luxury Hotels

An excellent hotel to stay at, on the more expensive side, is the Marriott Hotel. They are famously known for their superb dining, excellent service, elegant rooms, and comfortable beds. It is connected to the Convention Center. Another great choice is the Holiday Inn. This hotel is brand new and they have a fantastic bar, and excellent service. It is located less than one mile away from the airport and they offer free shuttle rides within a 5 mile radius. Another great aspect is that it is one mile away from the McAfee Coliseum.
