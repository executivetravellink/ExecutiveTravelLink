Making your Booking

Your booking will only be a confirmed booking when Executive Travel Link has issued a confirmation voucher referring to the relevant booking reference number. Executive Travel Link will only do this when it has received payment in full.

Before you book and before you travel it is also your responsibility to ensure that you and all those travelling with you have a valid passport and any necessary visa and that you have obtained any necessary vaccinations to gain entry to any country you are visiting. It can often take some time to obtain a passport or visa and therefore you should apply well in advance. In peak periods it is advisable to allow at least 4 weeks. Executive Travel Link will have no liability to you if you or any member of your party travels without the correct passport and visa or without the necessary vaccinations.

If so notified, Executive Travel Link will ask its suppliers to accommodate your needs. Executive Travel Link cannot guarantee that its suppliers will be able to accommodate your needs and is not in a position to require them to do so. Even though Executive Travel Link communicates your needs to its suppliers Executive Travel Link does not accept any liability to you for any failure by suppliers to satisfy your needs and will not be liable to you for any losses you may incur in the event that Executive Travel Link or its suppliers are unable to accommodate your requirements.

The time prior to travel and up until which bookings will be accepted by Executive Travel Link on your behalf is dependent on the supplier of the hotel accommodation or other travel component/s. Bookings for accommodation require at least one full name for each room. Please check your details carefully before you book as incorrect or incomplete details may result in the booking being cancelled and you may be charged a fee to cover any resulting administration costs incurred by Executive Travel Link in having to amend or cancel any bookings made by you.

Executive Travel Link reserves the right to refuse, at its sole discretion, any booking you seek to make through Executive Travel Link.

Complaints

Complaints regarding hotel accommodation or other supplier's services must be brought to the attention of the hotel or supplier's management as soon as possible during your stay. If the complaint is not resolved to your satisfaction, you must notify Executive Travel Link immediately on the telephone number provided on your Voucher.

If you consider that your complaint is not dealt with adequately at the time, you must notify Executive Travel Link as soon as possible in writing and in any event within (30) thirty days of your return, quoting your original booking reference and providing all relevant information about your complaint.

Please note that failure to follow the above procedure may reduce or extinguish any rights you have to claim compensation from Executive Travel Link, or from the relevant supplier. Any such rights will be reduced or extinguished particularly if, had you followed the above procedures, you or Executive Travel Link or its suppliers could have taken steps to reduce any loss or damage suffered or entirely prevented it from being suffered. Further, it is difficult and sometimes impossible properly to investigate a complaint if it is not notified reasonably quickly once your trip is over. Your rights to claim compensation may therefore also be reduced or extinguished, should any delay in your complaint being notified during or after your trip, prevent the carrying out of a proper investigation.
