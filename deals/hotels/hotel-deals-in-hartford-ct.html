Hartford, CT Features

Best known as “The Insurance Capital of the World,” Hartford, Connecticut is home to nearly 125,000 people. Rich with both historical and cultural backgrounds, Hartford boasts some of the nation’s oldest attractions, including the nation’s oldest public museum. From business to pleasure, Hartford should be your next travel destination. Housing several major insurance companies can mean serious business, but the city of Hartford has a lot of love for theatre and the arts. And you can visit the bustling businesses, trendy nightspots and historical attractions staying anywhere, from a hotel deal near the Hartford Convention Center to the luxurious accommodations downtown.

Getting Around in Hartford, CT

Whether you are staying at a budget hotel in Hartford or a five-star luxury resort, getting around the town is crucial to an enjoyable stay. Fortunately the CTTransit system provides a bus system to access all areas of Hartford and beyond in the Connecticut community. You can visit their website for bus schedules and trip planning. Also keep an eye out for the new, environmentally friendly buses operating in the greater Hartford area. These oversized buses allow for more passenger seating, an accordion-style midsection for easier turns in the city streets and above all, decreased pollutants to the air due to its hybrid technology.
With the right information and accommodations on a budget that you are willing spend; your trip to Hartford can be memorable for its relaxation and fun, whether the visit is for business or pleasure.

Hotel Deals in Hartford, CT

Discount Hotels

If you are looking to stay in one of the hotel deals in Hartford, you have a number of options. The Econo Lodge at 490 Main Street in East Hartford is a pet-friendly hotel just minutes from the downtown area, with accommodations for families and business travelers alike. Rates begin around $50 per night, depending on dates of travel.
The Holiday Inn Express Hotel and Suites prides itself on providing full amenities on a budget, which include ample space for meetings, a factor that is particularly attractive to business travelers. It is conveniently located near The Hartford Convention Center and offers packages starting around $85 per night.

Mid-Range Hotels

If you are looking to spend just a bit more of the budget on your accommodations, The Hilton Hartford hotel has much to offer. With beautifully furnished suites and a trendy full-service bar come nightfall, this hotel knows the needs of the modern tourist and business traveler. Rates run from $104 per night.
The Ramada East Hartford hotel offers reasonably priced simplicity during your stay, including conveniences such as free-internet in their public areas, an indoor swimming pool and fitness center and access to laundry services. And for around $99 per night, your stay at The Ramada can be easily arranged.

Luxury Hotels

If you are looking for a bit of luxury, book The Marriot Hartford Downtown, a spacious location with a scenic view overlooking the Connecticut River. Providing up-scale dining options, a fitness center and spa, The Marriot is ideal for comfort and relaxation during your stay. Rates vary, beginning at $159 per night.
But for something with a classic New England style during your stay in Connecticut, the Farmington Inn is a mere ten minutes from the Hartford area. With daily breakfast buffets and access to the Farmington Country Club, state-of-the-art accommodations for business conferences and a bounty of seasonally appropriate outdoor activities from golf to fishing, the Farmington Inn is an elegant option for both families and business functions. Rates start around $150 per night, depending on room availability.
